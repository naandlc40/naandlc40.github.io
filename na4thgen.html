This picture was taken around 1860. So glad someone in the family managed to save it. I received it from uncle Roly. Thanks to him we have this picture. Weird when you think it is now 140 years old.
I have seen his name reported as Naber for some reason but the church registries I have accessed say Nabor.
The story I was told is that he went to the Kamouraska region for a period of time. Not surprising as his mom's family was from there but he apparently moved back to the St Godefroi - Paspébiac area. Records do indicate that several of his children were born there.
             Nabor Anglehart

 

His wife: Josephine Quessy
As previously mentioned : could be Caissie, Caissy, or Casey
In those days a lot of names were spelled phonetically and that accounts the so many reported spellings.
She was related to the Hall-Kelly families.
