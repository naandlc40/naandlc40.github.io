The Source

Growing up I heard a lot of stories and wish I had questioned more and certainly retained more information but as I recall here is the information I remember
At that time there was great concern in Canada about an eventual conflict with the United States. There were a couple of incidences of that of course if you check into the history books.
The fact is that the government at the time tried to encourage retiring soldiers and mercenaries to retire here in Canada, figuring I guess that they would be useful in the event of armed conflict.
I heard that this was the scenario with Joseph and his son. They were soldiers or mercenaries and that they were granted land if they were to stay and live here and cleared and cultivated the land.

Sure wish I could find documentation on both the land grant and the actual occupations of Joseph and Jean-Baptiste.
