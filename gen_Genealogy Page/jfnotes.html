Jeanette Forget

I had so little information on my mother. She died so young I never did have a chance to really know her. I mean I knew my mom as a parent but we never had a chance to grow older together and become friends as well.
Lately though I have come across more precise information.
I have a copy now of her marriage certificate             

I had even less information on my maternal family until a few days ago. I found the name of my grandmother and grandfather Forget in a database. There are some errors there that hopefully can be corrected but there is no doubt that we are talking about the same people in these data entries. Looks like there were just some typos.

Errors or not I do wish to thank the fine people "Les Malenfant"          
for the considerable talent, effort and just plain hard work and dedication for accumulating this database and making all this information available on their websites.

The actual location of database entry is here:     

383 Child

of ERNEST MALENFANT and MARIE-FÉLENISE POITRAS is          

i.ALMA23 MALENFANT, m.ALBERT GORGET, 7 July 1917, Hanmer Ontario.

All this information seems to be correct except for the spelling: should read Forget instead of Gorget

If you can believe this they actually have traced grand-mère Alma Forget-Malenfant's ancestors 23 generations back going to the mid 1200. Incredible research folks.
